Send your offering via M-Pesa Paybill using the steps below.

How to pay via M-Pesa Paybill

  1. Open the M-Pesa menu on your phone.
  2. Select Lipa na M-Pesa.
  3. Choose Paybill.
  4. Enter Paybill Number: 836337
  5. Enter Account Name: Church Offering,tithe,project
  6. Enter Amount and confirm.

Open the M-Pesa app

Tap the M-Pesa logo to go to the app page on your phone.
