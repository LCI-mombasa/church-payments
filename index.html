How to pay via M-Pesa Paybill

  1. Open M-Pesa: Go to the M-Pesa menu on your phone.
  2. Select Lipa na M-Pesa: Choose the Paybill option.
  3. Enter Paybill Number: 836337
  4. Enter Account: Church Offering, tithe, project
  5. Enter Amount: Confirm to complete.

Open the M-Pesa app

Tap the M-Pesa button below to open the app or install it if needed.
